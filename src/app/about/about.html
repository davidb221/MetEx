How to Schedule a Delivery

Scheduling a delivery with MetEx Delivery is simple and convenient. You can contact our customer service team via phone or email, and they will assist you in arranging your delivery based on your specific requirements. During the scheduling process, you’ll need to provide essential details such as pick-up and drop-off addresses, package dimensions, and any special handling instructions. Once your delivery is scheduled, you will receive confirmation from your customer service representitive and we will take care of the rest from there.
